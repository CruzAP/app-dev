# Yahari Ore no Seishun wa Machigatteiru

## 📺 Overview
**Stranger Things** is a romantic comedy anime adapted from the same title of  novel by Watru Watri. It follows the social life of the protagonist Hikigaya Hachiman--a cynical high-schooler who strugles with his relationship with people. The story starts with his involuntary application on Volunteer's Club where he met Yukinoshita Yukino, a girl like him but also different. The story is rich with comedic reliefs and serious notes that delves deep on human relationships.

- **Genre**: Comedy, Romance, Slice of Life  
- **Created by**: The Duffer Brothers  
- **First Aired**: July 15, 2016  
- **Streaming on**: Netflix  

## 🌟 Main Characters
- **Hikigaya Hachiman** – A cyncical boy that struggles with human relationships.
- **Yukinoshita Yukino** – An ice-cold woman that also struggles with human relationships.
- **Yuigahama Yui** – A bubbly girl--classmate of Hachiman--who is also a member of Volunteer's Cub.

## 🎯 Why I Love It
- Good plot 
- Strong emotional depth and character development  
- I like the setting and the storywritting of the show.  

## 🔍 Favorite Episode
**Season 2, Episode 8 – “But Still, Hikigaya Hachiman Is...”**  
This episode is where Hachiman broke down and let his emotions out, an unexpected action from him that made him more understood by both the characters and the viewers.
